EntSoc aims to build inclusive innovation by exposing students of all faculties to Waterloo's entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Our Values

Support Entrepreneurship

We aim to spread an entrepreneurial mindset at UW by helping students navigate through the ecosystem and access resources.

Promote Inclusivity

We strive to promote collaboration in the entrepreneurial ecosystem by building bridges between students and various stakeholders on campus.

Build Connections

We strive to promote collaboration in the ecosystem by building bridges between organizations and individuals.

Provide Opportunities

We challenge students to step out of their comfort zones, connect with others, and unlock their potential for growth and self-development.

Our Events

Every term, we run events to connect students to entrepreneurial resources on campus as well as startups in the Kitchener-Waterloo area. Stay tuned with events from our campus partners as well!

UW Net. is a joint discord server that we made in collaboration with other tech and entrepreneurial related clubs on campus. Join to stay up-to-date on opportunities and meet like-minded UW students!

Partners

Our partners provide many resources and opportunities for UW students, and each bring unique value to the entrepreneurial ecosystem. We collaborate with our partners on events, internal projects, promotion, and to fill gaps within the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Projects

Centralizing on building bridges and acting on opportunities within the entrepreneurial ecosystem at UW, projects are a huge part of what our core members work on during the term. If you're interested in any of these projects, please feel free to contact us.
